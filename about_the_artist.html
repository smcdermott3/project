Information about the artist

Kanye West

This Chicago, South Side native started off as a producer, but burst on the scene with his 2003 album "College Dropout." Over the years, West has been known for both his crazy antics yet musical genius. West has been responsible for many musical evolutions throughout Hip-Hop, but is most often revered for his pioneering in perfecting sampling. West also is able to stand apart from the crowd by offering consistently new sounds for each album, from Yeezus' industrial cacophony to Graduation's soulful mix, West is always bringing new sounds to the public's attention.

YG

Perhaps the leading force of Gangsta Rap right now, YG is from Compton, California and an outspoken gang member of the Bloods. With his previous experience in gang activity, YG is able to draw listeners in to the gang-banging mentality through his songs. Though YG has been making moves into other sounds - slower or more party tunes - he is still unmistakeably YG.

Jack Harlow

Jack Harlow burst onto the scene with "What's Poppin," but Jack Harlow has been on the rise even before that. This rapper comes from Louisville, Kentucky, and is representing the 502 well. He's funny and quirky, yet still manages to go in hard on his beats. His rapping ability has gained him respect in the Hip-Hop game, and is one of the best new rappers of the past couple of years.

dvsn

Toronto, Canada is perhaps one of the most successful cities in terms of Hip-Hop outside of the United States, and one group that comes out of Toronta is dvsn. Signed to Drake's OVO Sounds, the singer Daniel Daley and producer Nineteen85 has made plenty of hits with thick bass and smooth vocals. Strangely, dvsn is one of those groups that many people know of, and admire, but don't follow too closely.

Action Bronson

Bronson has one of the most unique come-ups in Hip-Hop. Starting in New York, Bronson was actually a gourmet chef previous to rapping. An injury in the kitchen then inspired a career change, and Bronson began rapping full-time. His lyrics, fittingly, is full of colorful food descriptions. Yet, what stands out the most about him is his humor through his songs, which never fail to entertain. Bronson is certainly niche, but those who enjoy him thoroughly do.

Manila Grey

The rising duo hails from Manila, Philippines but now resides in Vancouver, Canada. This duo is making moves in the underground and gaining popularity fast, with hits starting to come into the mainstream. The group came into existence in 2016 by the merger of Filipinos Neeko and Soliven, whose voices both contrast and compliment each other on spacey beats.

Kendrick Lamar

Hailed as the best rapper of this generation, Lamar is able to talk about many issues spanning from race relations, spirituality, gang life, poverty, and more. Lamar is the only rapper to win a Pulitzer Prize for his album "Damn.", but his works from Section 80 on have talked about a multitude of issues with complex lyricism. His debut studio album, "good kid, m.A.A.d. city" brought him into the mainstream with a concept album that has been one of the most proclaimed Hip-Hop albums ever, and is often titled as a modern day classic.
