Who am I?

My name is Steven and, as you can guess, I'm using this website to review music. I am an avid Hip-Hop listener and enjoy many sub-genres. My ratings are, of course, subjective, but I try my best to inject objectivity into the reviews. For many, music is an emotional venture and it is no different with me. So, while I try to be objective, I can't always.
